Chapter 6 Local Government Policies

Lecture Preview

Learning Objectives

After this week, students will be able to:

  • Describe major-types of land-use regulations and the policy problems that they are trying to solve
  • Apply basic economic reasoning to analyze how land-use regulations affect housing supply, prices, and development patterns
  • Assess empirical evidence on the consequences of land-use regulations
  • Consider other ways in which state and local policy drive urban and housing policy
