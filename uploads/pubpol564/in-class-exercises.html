Let’s think about the effects of land-use regulations. There’s a lot of ways to model these, but I’m going to start by thinking of land-use regulations as capping the number of units that developers can build. (This may seem strange, but consider minimum lot sizes, one of the most important land-use regulations. Suppose you’re a developer and you have 100,000 square feet on which to build. If the government imposes a minimum lot size of 5,000 square feet, you can build at most 20 units on that land.)
