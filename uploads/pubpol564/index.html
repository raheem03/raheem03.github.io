Welcome

Welcome to PUBPOL 564, Housing and Social Policy. This page hosts lecture notes and some content associated with the course.

How to Use These Notes

Before class

Each week, you are required to complete the lecture preview associated with a lecture before class. In these previews, I will typically:

  • Articulate learning objectives
  • Justify assigned readings and provide guiding questions
  • Provide some notes or interactive exercises to get you thinking about the topic

You are also required to do any assigned reading before class. I will primarily ask you to focus on readings that enrich or complement the lecture and our discussions. Each week in the lecture preview, I will provide some justification for the readings and some guiding questions you may consider as you read.

Each week, these lecture previews may come with additional discussion questions for you to consider, beyond those presented with each of the readings.

Many weeks, the lecture preview will also come with come with some interactive questions. These are meant to help motivate the upcoming lecture and to get you thinking about the topic. After you complete these, note whether your guesses were accurate or not. If not, think about why you were wrong, and how the correct information might impact your beliefs.

Take the discussion questions and the interactive questions seriously. I strongly encourage you to write down your reflections. We may discuss any of this in person, either as a class or in small groups.

After class

You are required to review the lecture notes after class. I also encourage you to review the supplementary reading.
